Welcome to NYC Panache Salon, located in the heart of Forest Hills, Queens. We look forward to making your time here like no other. We provide the highest quality of services to all our guests.

Our salon ensures a personal consultation to help the stylist understand and achieve the client's desired look. We provide a variety of hair cutting techniques — point cutting, razor cuts, slicing, channeling, slide cutting and twist cutting — along with styling techniques such as Sleek & Smooth, Beach Waves, Flip Out & Volume Blowout.

We specialize in Hair Cuts, Blow Drys, Up-Dos, Color Corrections, Highlights, Balayage, Ombré, 1-Day Keratin Treatments, Brazilian Blow-Outs, Pro-Addiction Smoothing, Body Waves, Hair Extensions, Nails, Full Body Waxing, Massage and much more.
