Marina Iskiyaeva

Founder & Master Stylist

For over 25 years, Marina Iskiyaeva has dedicated her life to the art of hair — mastering cuts, color, and transformative styling that brings out the confidence in every client who sits in her chair.

What began as a deep passion for beauty has evolved into NYC Panache Salon, a luxury destination in Forest Hills, Queens, where every detail is intentional and every visit feels like an indulgence. Marina's philosophy is simple: beauty should never feel rushed, and excellence should never be compromised.

At Panache, the experience goes beyond hair. From precision nail artistry to flawless waxing, every service is delivered with the same meticulous care and attention to detail that Marina has spent decades perfecting.
